05

Causal structure (DAG)

A directed acyclic graph showing the assumed causal relationships in the model. Arrow thickness on the controls → rent path reflects that confounders are strongly significant. The bike lane → rent arrow is dashed because the coefficient (β = 5.71) is not statistically significant at p = 0.55 — consistent with the cross-sectional null result. The endogeneity path (controls → bike lanes) represents the placement concern that wealthier neighborhoods attract both bike infrastructure and higher rents — addressed here by the long-difference design.
